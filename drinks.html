Drinks & Desserts

No street-food crawl is complete without something sweet or a refreshing drink. Here are local favorites:

Tatara Ice Cream Stands

Location: Liberty Square
Specialty: Homemade fruit sorbets and creamy yogurt flavors.

Dry Bridge Churchkhela

Location: Dry Bridge Market
Specialty: Handmade churchkhela with walnuts, hazelnuts, and grape must.

Old Tbilisi Chacha

Location: Meidan Square
Specialty: Traditional grape brandy (chacha) served cold in shot glasses.

Georgian Berry Kompot

Location: Rustaveli Ave
Specialty: Refreshing compote made from local berries (served iced).
