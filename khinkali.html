Famous Khinkali Corners in Tbilisi

Juicy, dumpling-style khinkali are a must-try. Below are our top 4 picks:

Mtatsminda Khinkali Corner

Location: Mtatsminda Plateau
Specialty: Pork & beef khinkali served piping hot, paired with tarragon sauce.

Dry Bridge Khinkali Stand

Location: Dry Bridge Flea Market
Specialty: Mushroom khinkali for vegetarians, perfected dough.

Sololaki Gourmet Khinkali

Location: Sololaki District
Specialty: Lamb khinkali with spicy peppercorn reduction.

Vera Square Khinkali

Location: Vera Neighborhood
Specialty: Mini-khinkali sampler platter (pork, beef, cheese).
