Top Khachapuri Stalls in Tbilisi

Khachapuri is one of Georgia’s most iconic dishes. Here are our 4 must-visit stalls:

Adjarian Khachapuri Haven

Location: Old Town
Specialty: Cheese-filled boat khachapuri topped with egg and butter.

Varketili Family Khachapuri

Location: Varketili Market
Specialty: Kvemi kartuli (plain cheese) and Megruli khachapuri (extra cheese topping).

Rustaveli Square Bakery

Location: Rustaveli Ave
Specialty: Imeruli khachapuri (round, thin dough) and cheese layering.

Sololaki Street Bakery

Location: Sololaki District
Specialty: Traditional Georgian cheese & sulguni cheese mix.
